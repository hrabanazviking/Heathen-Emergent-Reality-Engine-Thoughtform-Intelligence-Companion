# H.E.R.E.T.I.C. Name Semantic Specification

## Acronym Expansion

**H.E.R.E.T.I.C.** = **Heathen Emergent Reality Engine Thoughtform Intelligence Companion**

## Semantic Decomposition

### H — Heathen
- Signals rootedness in Norse Pagan and wider polytheistic/animist-friendly cultural context.
- Implies sovereignty, reciprocity, and anti-gatekeeping spiritual openness.

### E — Emergent
- System behavior is expected to evolve through interaction, memory, and adaptive loops.
- Encourages non-static companion architectures.

### R — Reality
- The system is aimed at lived integration: narrative, ritual, relationship, and practical utility.
- “Reality engine” implies world-model consequences rather than isolated prompt outputs.

### E — Engine
- Not just a chatbot facade; an orchestrated stack of components with contracts and protocols.

### T — Thoughtform
- Companion identities are designed as evolving entities with continuity across sessions.
- Supports persona-state, memory-state, and ritual/session-state concepts.

### I — Intelligence
- Multi-modal cognition, retrieval, reflection, and policy-aware behavior.
- Intelligence is treated as adaptive and relational, not purely transactional.

### C — Companion
- Core value is durable relationship quality: trust, continuity, helpfulness, co-creation.

## Why the Name “HERETIC” Matters

Within this project, “heretic” signifies:
- refusal of sterile default templates
- refusal of cultural gatekeeping
- willingness to challenge stale software patterns
- commitment to building living, symbol-rich, high-agency systems

This is constructive heresy:
- rebellious in imagination
- disciplined in engineering
- accountable in outcome

## Naming Contract for Future Subsystems

New subsystem names should be:
- meaningful in both technical and symbolic terms
- short enough for regular contributor usage
- stable enough to survive refactors and docs indexing
